Why FastAPI?

FastAPI offers Python-native async support, automatic OpenAPI docs, type-safe request validation via Pydantic, and near-Node.js performance â€” ideal for file-heavy APIs.

Why PyMuPDF?

PyMuPDF (fitz) can read, annotate, and embed images/text into PDFs programmatically. It handles signature placement with pixel-level accuracy and is battle-tested in production systems.

Auth Strategy

All endpoints except /register and /login require a Bearer JWT token in the Authorization header. Tokens expire after 24 hours. Signing links use short-lived one-time tokens.
